Biography

Hello! I’m Jairo Iqbal Gil, a 21-year-old Computer Science student at Hunter College residing in the dynamic borough of the Bronx. I work as a Help Desk Technician, where I specialize in providing technical support and resolving user issues. My responsibilities range from troubleshooting hardware and software problems to keeping meticulous records of technical incidents.

Managing my academic workload alongside my job presents its challenges, but it’s incredibly rewarding. I am deeply committed to both my studies and my role in tech support. Beyond my professional and academic life, I relish exploring the diverse culture of the Bronx, engaging in various hobbies, and continually expanding my knowledge in the ever-evolving field of computer science.
